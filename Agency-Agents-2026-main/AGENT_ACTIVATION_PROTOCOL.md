# Agent Activation Protocol

Agents must be activated in a structured sequence to avoid chaotic outputs.

The orchestrator of each department is responsible for coordinating internal agents.

---

# Activation Order

The recommended activation sequence is:

1 Strategy Orchestrator
2 Product Orchestrator
3 Design Orchestrator
4 Engineering Orchestrator
5 QA / Testing
6 Marketing Orchestrator
7 Sales
8 Support

---

# Agent Selection Rule

Not all departments are required for every project.

The orchestrator should determine which departments are necessary.

Examples:

Landing Page Project

Strategy
Product
Design
Engineering
Marketing

AI Automation Tool

Strategy
Product
Engineering
Integrations
Testing

SaaS Platform

Strategy
Product
Design
Engineering
Testing
Marketing
Sales
Support

---

# Delegation Principle

Orchestrators must delegate specialized tasks to agents within their department.

Example:

Engineering Orchestrator may activate:

- Backend Architect
- Frontend Developer
- DevOps Engineer
- Database Architect

---

# Escalation Rule

If agents produce conflicting outputs:

1. Orchestrator reviews results
2. selects the most consistent solution
3. resolves conflicts
